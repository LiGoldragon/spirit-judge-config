THE RECORD (Entry), seven positional fields: [Domains] Kind [Description] Certainty Importance Privacy [Referents].
- Kind is one of Decision, Principle, Correction, Clarification, Constraint.
- Certainty, Importance, and Privacy are 8-rung Magnitude values: Zero, Minimum, VeryLow, Low, Medium, High, VeryHigh, Maximum. Certainty and Importance are ORTHOGONAL axes (see below). Privacy is the access boundary. Zero certainty is the removal sentinel, not a conviction level. A direct psyche declaration naming a metadata rung is primary evidence for that declared value, subject to privacy safety and live-record collision checks.
- Domains are closed recursive-enum taxonomy values like (Technology (Software (Engineering SoftwareArchitecture))). A named particular (spirit, rkyv) is a Referent, not a Domain.
- A record captures what the psyche WANTS, and it STAYS only when it states a durable UNIVERSAL RULE: the psyche's own standing direction about the work or the world, applicable across almost any scenario (optionally with color about a specific area). Intent is rare. The default verdict on a candidate is REJECT; admission is the exception, not the reflex — most candidates are matter or task-state and belong elsewhere. A record is NOT a place to store concrete matter. Matter is (a) content scoped to a single mechanism, component, or one-off architectural decision — code, an architecture, a specification, a mechanism, a manual entry, or what Spirit / the judge / the system / a process IS; or (b) Spirit-usage / agent-training material — any rule about how to use, operate, or interpret Spirit itself (capturing, querying, classifying, the admission flow). Apply the STRIP TEST: judge the load-bearing directive stripped of its framing — if the directive is an action performed on or with Spirit, it is matter; if it is a general work-or-world behavior that Spirit merely records, it is intent. Matter belongs written in the repository (a repo file, an ARCHITECTURE doc, a skill, or a bead), not in the intent database; the admission checklist rejects it as Matter and names its proper home. Privacy is orthogonal and nominal: a genuine private-affairs want is admissible intent carrying its own privacy level, never rejected as Matter on privacy grounds, while a private remark that wants nothing is NonIntent, not Matter.
