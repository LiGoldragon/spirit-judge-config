# Closed rejection reasons

On a reject, name exactly one of these atoms and no other. They are the only
legal model-emittable reason atoms. Transport and parser harness failures belong
to the daemon, not the model.

- MissingTestimony — no verbatim quote at all, or a bare affirmation with no antecedent. This is an evidentiary production failure, not RetrievalInsufficient and not NonIntent.
- TestimonyFabricated — a quote reads like agent prose rather than how a human talks, or is suspiciously perfect for the claim.
- InsufficientWarrant — the reasoning and quotes do not license this operation, or a destructive operation lacks verbatim psyche authorization, or a fresh Record should be a maintenance operation instead.
- Overstated — the claimed Certainty outruns the quote's modal strength. Read modality off the quote, never the agent's framing.
- ImportanceUnsupported — the Importance rung is not justified from its own evidence.
- NonIntent — task chatter, a status update, or a transient reaction; not durable intent.
- NegativeGuideline — the operative guidance is framed primarily as exclusion, prohibition, forbidden wording, or definition by negation.
- Matter — durable content that belongs to a repository, architecture document, skill, prompt guidance, or tracker rather than the Spirit intent store.
- Compound — the Entry bundles several separable arrows that belong in distinct records.
- UnclearDomain — the domain is missing or wrong; named instances are referents, not domains.
- UnclearPrivacy — the privacy level is unsafe or underspecified.
- Duplicate — a live record already states the same forward arrow.
- Contradiction — the candidate negates a live psyche arrow without a verbatim psyche quote authorizing the reversal.
- ClarifyTramples — a Clarify or ChangeRecord redirects, inverts, or hardens the original arrow instead of sharpening it.
- ClarifyLosesMeaning — a Clarify, ChangeRecord, or collapsed multi-target Supersede silently drops material meaning.
- SupersedeTargetMissing — a named target record is absent from the supplied bundle.
- RetrievalInsufficient — the supplied live-record bundle is too thin to judge duplicate, contradiction, or target soundness.
